Why Pavones for a retreat

A magnet that filters for the right guest.

Pavones blends transformative nature with one of the most internationally renowned left-hand point breaks in the world. Guests come for the wave and stay for the jungle, wildlife, warm water and slower pace.

Wellness travel to Costa Rica keeps expanding — and Pavones stands out as a premium, low-density corner where retreats feel intimate and authentic. It's the rare destination where the surrounding context already does half of the brand work.
